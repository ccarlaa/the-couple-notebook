$$

* $\mathbb{K}$ is the underlying field (e.g., $\mathbb{R}$, $\mathbb{C}$, etc.),
* $\mathbf{0}_V$ is the zero vector in the vector space $V$,
* $v_1, \dots, v_n \in V$ are given vectors.
